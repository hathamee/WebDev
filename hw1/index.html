All her albums

  • Taylor Swift
    • This is her debut album. It features some famous singles such as "Should've said no" and "picture to burn"
  • Fearless
    • This was her second album (She's since rerecorded it). It features uber famous songs like "Love story" and "You belong with me"
  • Speak Now
    • This is her third album. It features "Mine" and my personal favorite: "Back to December"
  • Red
    • This was her transition album from country to pop (also rerecorded). It features fan favorite "22" and "We are never ever getting back together"
  • 1989
    • This was her first fully pop album. You might regnize of of 5 top 10 charting singles such as "Out of the Woods", "blank space", or "Shake it off"
  • Reputation
    • A contraversial album amidst a dark time in her life. It features "Look what you made me do" as well as "King of My Heart" and "Getaway Car"
  • Lover
    • An adorable album with pop hits like "The man" and "Lover"
  • Folklore
    • Released during the pandemic, this album touches into a more poetic vibe. Some notable songs are "August", and "Exhile ft. Bon Iver"
  • Evermore
    • Folklore's sister album. It opens with "Willow" and contains "Champagne Problems" and the bonus track "Right where you left me"
